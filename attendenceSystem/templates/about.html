About Our Smart Attendance System

Welcome to our innovative Smart Attendance System. Our system provides a secure and efficient way for students to mark their attendance during offline classes. Leveraging advanced technologies, our platform ensures real-time attendance tracking with additional layers of security. Students can conveniently mark their presence in the presence of a teacher, utilizing features such as location verification and facial recognition for enhanced security.

Key Features:

  • Real-time attendance tracking: The system captures attendance data in real-time, providing accurate and up-to-date records.
  • Location verification for on-site attendance: Utilizing location services, our system verifies that students are present in the designated classroom for accurate attendance marking.
  • Facial recognition for enhanced security: Facial recognition technology adds an extra layer of security, ensuring that the attendance is marked by the authorized student.
  • Proxy prevention measures: Our system incorporates robust measures to prevent proxy attendance, ensuring the integrity of attendance records.
